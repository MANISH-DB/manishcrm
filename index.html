How It Works

From lead to booking — tracked at every step.

1

Lead Comes In

Admin adds new lead with name, mobile, source, project and assigns to telecaller. Duplicate mobile detection runs automatically.

2

Telecaller Makes Contact

Telecaller updates status — Not Connect → First Contact → Share Brochure → Follow-up → Ready to Visit. Every update is time-stamped in Call History.

3

Site Visit is Planned

Lead moves to SV Plan. Admin assigns Sales person. Sales team takes over with their own dashboard — SV Plan → SV Done.

4

Booking Done ✓

Sales updates status to Booking Done → Registration Done. Admin sees full conversion funnel in Reports. Every deal tracked end-to-end.
